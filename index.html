F.A.Q

Trade FAQ

Common questions regarding our Import/Export operations.

01

Do you have a Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)?

Yes, as an Import/Export company, we primarily deal with B2B wholesale. MOQs vary by product category (e.g., accessories have higher MOQs than smart home devices). Please contact us for a specific quote.

02

Can you source specific laptops or phones?

While our main inventory focuses on accessories and smart home gadgets, we can source specific models of laptops and phones for bulk orders through our partner network.

03

What shipping terms do you offer?

We offer standard Incoterms such as FOB, CIF, and EXW. We can arrange air freight for urgent orders or sea freight for cost-effective bulk shipping.
